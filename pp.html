Privacy Policy

Your trust is our top priority.

1. Introduction

At Rural Retreats, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you use our website and services. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services to you. The types of data we collect include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details when you make a booking or contact us.
  • Payment Information: We collect payment details (credit card numbers, etc.) to process transactions. This data is handled by secure third-party payment processors.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with our website, such as IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited, to analyze trends and improve our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your bookings.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • To personalize your experience and show you relevant content.
  • To improve our website's functionality and services.
  • To send you promotional emails and updates about our services (with your consent).

4. Data Security

We take security seriously. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Our website is scanned on a regular basis for security vulnerabilities and we use encryption to protect sensitive data transmitted online.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also object to our use of your data or request that we restrict its use. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@ruralretreats.com.
