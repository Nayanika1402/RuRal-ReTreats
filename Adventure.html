Discover breathtaking hiking trails, from lush valleys to icy glaciers, and set out on your next adventure.

India is home to some of the most beautiful and diverse trekking trails, ranging from the snow-covered peaks of the Himalayas to the lush green Western Ghats. Whether you're an experienced trekker or a beginner, there's a trail for everyone. Here are some of the top hiking trails that promise adventure, scenic beauty, and an unforgettable experience.

Hampta Pass Trek, Himachal Pradesh

A scenic trek connecting Kullu Valley to Lahaul, featuring glaciers, meadows, and pristine rivers.

  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best Time: June - September
  • Elevation: 14,100 ft
  • Duration: 5-6 days
Learn More

Kudremukh Trek, Karnataka

A lush green trail in the Western Ghats, famous for its rolling hills and diverse wildlife.

  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best Time: September - February
  • Elevation: 6,207 ft
  • Duration: 2 days
Learn More

Chadar Trek, Ladakh

A thrilling frozen river trek on the Zanskar River, offering an adventurous winter challenge.

  • Difficulty: Difficult
  • Best Time: January - February
  • Elevation: 11,100 ft
  • Duration: 8-9 days
Learn More

Sandakphu Trek, West Bengal

The highest point in West Bengal, offering breathtaking views of the world's tallest peaks.

  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best Time: April - May, October - December
  • Elevation: 11,930 ft
  • Duration: 6-7 days
Learn More

Valley of Flowers, Uttarakhand

A UNESCO World Heritage site known for vibrant flowers and Himalayan beauty.

  • Difficulty: Easy to Moderate
  • Best Time: July - September
  • Elevation: 14,100 ft
  • Duration: 4-6 days
Learn More

Kedarnath Trek, Uttarakhand

A sacred journey through the Himalayas to the revered Kedarnath Temple.

  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best Time: May - October
  • Elevation: 11,755 ft
  • Duration: 3-4 days
Learn More
